# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 7, 2026

Twerkout is designed to protect your privacy. **Twerkout does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.** Your workout data is stored locally on your device.

### Data We Collect
Twerkout **does not collect or send any app data to our servers**, because we don’t operate any servers for Twerkout.

### Data Storage
- **On-device only:** Your workouts, exercises, and session history are stored on your iPhone (and may be included in your personal device backups, depending on your iOS settings).
- **No third-party analytics or ads:** Twerkout does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that transmit your data off-device.

### Apple Health & Apple Watch Integration
If you choose to connect Twerkout with **Apple Health** and/or **Apple Watch**, Twerkout may **read and/or write fitness-related data** through Apple’s HealthKit framework (for example, saving workouts to Health).

- **Optional:** You can use Twerkout without enabling Apple Health/Watch integration.
- **You control access:** You can grant or revoke access at any time in the Health app or in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health**.
- **Apple handles Health data:** Health data you share with Apple Health is managed by Apple according to your device and iCloud settings.

### Data Sharing
- **No third-party sharing:** Twerkout does not share your data with third parties.
- **No external servers:** Twerkout does not upload your workouts or personal data to any external server.

### Your Choices
You can:
- Disable Apple Health/Watch access at any time in iOS settings.
- Delete the app to remove locally stored data from your device (subject to any device backup/restore behavior you control).

### Children’s Privacy
Twerkout is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes to This Policy
If this privacy policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
